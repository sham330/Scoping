02

Summary Preliminary Project Studies (APS)

The objectives of the preliminary design study are to specify the general composition of the construction (plan, interior volumes and exterior appearance), and to propose the technical solutions to be considered.

Study Components:

  • Architectural layout and design concepts
  • Space optimization and flow analysis
  • Exterior appearance and aesthetics
  • Technical solution recommendations
More details
03

Detailed Preliminary Project Studies (APD)

The preliminary design study goes further than the preliminary design study. The plans, sections, facades, and dimensions of the building are detailed, the choice of materials is determined, etc. The study also consists of defining the installations and equipment to be installed.

Detailed Specifications:

  • Comprehensive building plans and sections
  • Material selection and specifications
  • Equipment and installation planning
  • Precise dimensional requirements
